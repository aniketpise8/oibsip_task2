Iron Man - The Armored Avenger
In the vast landscape of comic book superheroes, one name shines brighter than most: Iron Man. Created by writer and editor Stan Lee, scripter Larry Lieber, and artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby, Iron Man made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. With his iconic suit of armor, ingenious inventions, and unwavering resolve, Iron Man has captured the hearts of fans worldwide, both on the pages of comics and the silver screen of the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU). But it was the portrayal of Tony Stark/Iron Man by Robert Downey Jr. that transformed this character into a global icon and set a new standard for superhero storytelling.
